Welcome to VibraWise

We are creating a software suite that aims to address both nonlinear and linear vibrations in thin plates and shells. This comprehensive solution employs a diverse range of mathematical methods and theoretical approaches to ensure accurate and effective analyses.

About VibraWise

VibraWise is an extensive software suite designed for the analysis and simulation of vibrations in thin plates and shells. Our software integrates advanced mathematical techniques, such as numerical and FEM methods, to provide accurate and comprehensive solutions. The concept for VibraWise emerged during my research on shells. While exploring various shell dimensions, material properties, and boundary conditions, I realized the need to adapt different theoretical approaches and solution methods, often resulting in one-time-use code. Through extensive experience with multiple methods and software for problem-solving, the initial idea crystallized into creating a specialized software solution for linear and nonlinear shell and plate vibration analysis. Recognizing the challenges of programming expertise among scientists and students, I embarked on the mission to develop a user-friendly software solution. This software is engineered to be fast, precise, and accessible on standard computing systems, streamlining the process of scientific code writing and analysis.software is primarily developed using Python as a framework and integrates FORTRAN and C++ in certain processes to enhance the speed and efficiency of calculations.

Our Software

In version 0.9 of the release, we are pleased to offer the following modules:

  • Ready functions or series adopted for solving, such as Chebyshev polynomials.
  • Input basis function and coordinates.
  • Ready process for orthogonalization to orthogonalize any function, either from the template or inputted by the user.
  • Automatic linear part solving using FEM methods, including selecting the appropriate meshing method, suitable elements, and generating comparison results and graphs.
